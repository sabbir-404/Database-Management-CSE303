About Meat Grid

Meat Grid is a centralized digital platform developed to enhance transparency, coordination, and decision-making across the meat production and supply chain. Our system connects stakeholders including farmers, wholesalers, slaughterhouses, retailers, policymakers, researchers, and health authorities under one integrated environment.

Purpose and Objectives

The platform is designed to improve the accuracy and efficiency of meat supply and demand analysis in Bangladesh. By collecting, managing, and visualizing real-time data from various actors in the supply chain, Meat Grid supports data-driven operations and long-term planning. This ensures product quality, safety, affordability, and accessibility for all.

Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a more transparent and sustainable meat ecosystem that benefits all contributors to the supply chain. Through the use of modern technologies and a user-centric approach, Meat Grid enables better monitoring, efficient forecasting, and timely interventions based on accurate supply-demand trends and nutritional indicators.

Who We Serve

Our platform is tailored to meet the diverse needs of users across the meat industry:

  • Farmers tracking livestock and production efficiency
  • Slaughterhouses reporting processing rates and output
  • Retailers managing inventory and pricing data
  • Wholesalers monitoring bulk storage and distribution
  • Policy makers and researchers accessing macro-level analysis
  • Health professionals analyzing nutritional impact on consumers
